Chicken Adobo

A stew that is cooked either by braising or simmering. Its tangy flavors come from the basic ingredients of garlic, vinegar, crushed peppercorns, soy sauce, broth, bay leaves, and a dash of salt. Sometimes, pork belly chunks can be added with the chicken, which elevates the dish to an even more superb level.
