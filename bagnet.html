Bagnet

A celebrated Filipino dish known for its crispy, golden exterior and tender, flavorful interior. Originating from the Ilocos region, this delicacy involves deep-frying seasoned pork belly twice to achieve its signature crunch and juiciness. The pork is marinated with garlic, salt, and pepper, then cooked meticulously to perfection. It's often served with a tangy vinegar-based dipping sauce or bagoong, enhancing its rich, savory taste. Bagnet is a staple at Filipino festivities, embodying a unique blend of textures and flavors that make it a beloved classic nationwide.
