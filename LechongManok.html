Lechong Manok

A popular Filipino roasted chicken dish, marinated in a blend of lemongrass, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and vinegar, giving it a savory, slightly tangy flavor. The chicken is traditionally cooked over charcoal, turning it slowly to achieve an evenly crisp skin and moist, tender meat. This method infuses the chicken with a smoky aroma, enhancing its natural flavors. Often served with a liver-based sauce or a simple vinegar and garlic dip, Lechon Manok is a favorite for everyday meals and special occasions alike, celebrated for its rich taste and aromatic, juicy qualities.
